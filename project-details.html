Detecting Fusarium Wilt in Banana Plant Using LLM

Fusarium Wilt is a fungal disease that affects banana plants, causing yellowing, wilting, and eventually plant death, leading to major crop losses. To address this, we designed an web application that combines a machine learning model with hardware sensors to detect early symptoms. Our project helps farmers take timely action, reducing crop damage and improving yield. A chatbot is also integrated for easy user interaction and guidance.

Animal Rescue Database Management

The Animal Rescue Database Management System is a web-based application designed for pet shops to efficiently manage and store a wide range of information. It includes data on pets, birds, available products, sales records, customer details, adoption information, and resumes. The system offers a user-friendly interface that simplifies data entry and retrieval. By replacing manual paperwork, it ensures faster access to information, minimizes errors, and improves the overall efficiency of shop operations.

By applying models like linear and logistic regression, it analyzes patterns and provides accurate predictions, helping in early diagnosis and better patient care.
